Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

RecipeScanner is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Data we collect

When you create an account we collect:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Password (stored as a bcrypt hash — never in plain text)
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Recipes, ingredients and prices you create

2. How we use your data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the RecipeScanner service: authentication, recipe storage, and cost calculation. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising purposes.

3. Data storage

Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted by Supabase (EU West — Paris). All connections use TLS encryption.

4. Third-party services

Recipe PDF content is sent to Groq (AI service) for text extraction. Ingredient names may be sent to Open Food Facts to retrieve price estimates. No personally identifiable information is included in these requests.

5. Your rights

You can delete your account at any time from Account Settings. This permanently removes all your data from our database.

6. Cookies

RecipeScanner does not use tracking cookies. Authentication tokens are stored in your browser's localStorage.
